Today marks the beginning of something big. We laid the foundation for what will become a comprehensive component library with carefully organized block categories.

## What we built

We established the complete infrastructure supporting multiple block categories:

- **Hero Sections** - High-impact landing page headers with various layouts
- **Feature Sections** - Showcase your product features with style
- **CTA Sections** - Drive user actions with compelling call-to-actions
- **And more categories coming soon**

## What's next

This is just the beginning. We'll be adding more block categories, components, and features in the coming weeks. Stay tuned!

- More section types
- Additional variations for existing categories
- New animation and interaction patterns
- And much more
